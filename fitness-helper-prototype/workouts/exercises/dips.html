  3. Day 1 - Upperbody
  4. Dips

  1. Get into position by placing your hand on the parallel bars.
  2. Lower yourself down with your arms, keep your head and shoulders straight, until you reach at least a 90-degree angle with your elbows.
  3. Keep your elbows tucked close to your body; don't let them splay out. Make sure to lower yourself slowly. Try to work against gravity.
  4. Push yourself back up to the starting position.
  5. Slowly lower yourself to the starting position while breathing in.
